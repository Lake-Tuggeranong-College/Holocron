Welcome to the **Holocron**, the centralized knowledge base and curriculum repository for Computing and IT subjects at **Lake Tuggeranong College**.

This repository serves as the backend for our digital classroom environment. All course materials, lab instructions, and technical notes are managed here and synchronized for a seamless learning experience.

## ✨ Why "The Holocron"?

In the *Star Wars* universe, a **Holocron** is an ancient, palm-sized device used by scholars and masters to store vast amounts of data, interactive lessons, and wisdom. They were often "information-dense" and could only be accessed by those with the right intent and training.

## 📚 Subject Portals

This repository contains structured content for the following units of study:

### 🤖 Robotics and Mechatronics
Exploration of autonomous systems, hardware-software integration, and mechanical design.
* **Key Topics:** Arduino-compatible programming, sensors, and circuit design.
* **Core Projects:** Smart house development and autonomous car systems.

### 🔒 Networking and Security
Deep dives into how the internet works and how to protect digital assets.
* **Key Topics:** TCP/IP models, routing and switching, cybersecurity ethics, penetration testing, and student-led group development.
* **Core Projects:** Home and enterprise systems, and Capture the Flag (CTF) games.

### 💻 Digital Technologies
The study of computational thinking, data analysis, and software development.
* **Key Topics:** Games development, software development, project management, student-led group development, and UI design.
* **Core Projects:** First person shooter and multiplayer shooter.

### 🎓 Certificate III in Information Technology
Vocational education focusing on industry-standard IT skills and workplace readiness.
* **Key Topics:** System architecture, PHP website development, and student-led group development.
* **Core Projects:** Docker containers, E-commerce shopfront, and social media platform.
