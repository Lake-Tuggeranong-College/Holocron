# Written Tutorial

In this assessment you will create a tutorial following a modified Diataxis framework that will teach future students how to complete an advanced part of your project. The intention is to publish this tutorial on the Holocron for future students to follow. 

The tutorial must be written in the Google Doc assigned to you through Google Classroom. Writing in a first-person perspective is not appropriate.

Your tutorial must include:

- A stated aim for what will be achieved by the end of the tutorial.
- What the reader will learn (educational goals)
- A step by step explanation of how to implement the tutorial.
- Evidence (Images, Screenshots, Flowcharts etc) from the project showing the development of the feature and making it easier to follow for the student.

Throughout the tutorial include analysis of how these additions fit into the project as a whole and improve the project.

**Note:**

- The topic of your tutorial is to be decided by the instructor two weeks before the submission date
- It is recommended that you seek feedback from your instructor a week before the submission date
- It is recommended to review previous assessment examples provided to you.

  

The total word count for this assessment is 750 (± 10%), excluding evidence (code, images etc)

**The use of images, videos, diagrams and/or graphs is highly recommended.**
