# Assessment Conditions

Submission of your assessment must be electronic. You must submit your assessment through Google Classroom.  If you hand in your assessment in any other format, it will not be considered to be submitted until it is received in the Google Classroom assignment.  

Late submissions will result in the loss of marks in line with BSSS policies. Extensions are granted in line with BSSS and Lake Tuggeranong College policies. No extensions will be granted on the due date.

## Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)  

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an important tool, and its use can enhance your academic responses. It is important that you use AI in a responsible and ethical way that aligns with the academic integrity policies of the Board of Senior Secondary Studies and Lake Tuggeranong College.  Please note that the methods in which you may use AI may vary by subjects, teacher, and assessment items. For this reason, it is important that you review the permitted AI table with every assessment item.  

In this assessment, the permitted and prohibited use of AI is:  

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Permitted|You are permitted to use AI in a manner that: <br><br>- A tool to prompt your thought process in discovering an issue.  <br>    <br>- A tool to help you understand the extent and arguments of an issue. <br>    <br>- Assists you to proofread and reflect on the quality of your work.  <br>    <br>- Provide suggestions for improvement on your work.  <br>    <br>- Refine your talking points.<br>    <br>- Generate code to use as examples of concepts, acknowledging it as a source in the bibliography and in-text referencing.|
|Prohibited|You are not permitted to use AI in this assessment in the following manner: <br><br>- Write any part of your original response or draft!  <br>    <br>- Any other manner not mentioned in the permitted AI use.|

## Academic Integrity  

All work submitted must be your own; while you may use your peers, family or artificial intelligence to improve your work, the concepts behind your work must be yours. All sources, ideas and quotes should be appropriately referenced. This may include the use of APA and in-text referencing techniques.

Failure to submit original work, alongside appropriate declarations will result in action being taken per BSSS plagiarism policy. If you require assistance with referencing, you should discuss it with your teacher, the teacher​ ​librarians, or visit [www.ltclibrary.com](http://www.ltclibrary.com).
