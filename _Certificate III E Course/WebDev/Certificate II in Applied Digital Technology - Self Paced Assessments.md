# Overview

> [!warning] Only required to complete the Certificate II in Information Technology

This unit is a single assessment task which involves a series of **self-paced tasks** that can be completed at any time before the end of the course. The competencies covered are **core** competencies for the ICT20120 Certificate III in Applied Digital Technology - therefore they are **required** to gain the qualification.

# Tasks

## Task 5.1 - Participate in sustainable work practices

### Task Overview

> [!warning] Only required to complete the Certificate II in Information Technology

In this practical task, you will step into the role of an administrative professional at ClassCorp's Canberra office to audit and optimise your workstation for safety and environmental sustainability. Before diving into the system portal, you will design a "Top 5 Safe & Green Rules" visual compliance guide, which you will then upload during the interactive simulation to resolve ergonomic hazards, calculate the digital energy footprint of active office hardware, and correctly classify office e-waste according to ACT environmental guidelines. By the end of this work cycle, you will export a verified spreadsheet compliance log for your assessor, demonstrating that you can run a safe, green, and legally compliant modern office workstation.
